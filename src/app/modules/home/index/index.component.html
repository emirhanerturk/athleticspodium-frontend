WELCOME TO MEDAL HEAVEN

Eight years ago, we started our journey digital publishing with an athletics dedicated web site & magazine in Turkey (Atletizm Dünyası). Our printed magazine closed in late 2014, but web site continued on its way until this year, along with @atletizmdunyasi on twitter, which is one of the most trusted micro news sources. Now, we’re opening a new chapter on our publication scale: Newly-formed data-driven athletics database: Athleticspodium.com

Athletics Podium is aiming to become the main hub for athletics info-seekers. While the site will be storing all distributed medals in the international scene, also aiming to compile encyclopedia notes on medal-winning heroes.

We’re in testing period now for a while. but you can trace thousands medals which distributed in 57 different championships from South America to Asia.
